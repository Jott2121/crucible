# Fresh-Fable post-lock verification read of DRAFT-v4 (2026-07-13)

Independent reviewer instance with no prior contact with the manuscript. Recomputed every
recomputable statistic (analyze.py, analyze_b.py, sensitivity_b.py rerun; receipts under runs/,
runs-b/, seeds/ re-summed independently), checked the deviations log, protocols, git/push
timeline, and the review arc. The overwhelming majority of the paper's numbers reproduce
exactly. Four findings block; the rest are small.

## BLOCKING

B1. "one same-lineage Critic reply hit the ... raised output cap" -- receipts show THREE.
Experiment 2's counted receipts contain three truncation-rejected same-lineage critic rounds,
all at exactly 32,000 output tokens: graph-guard loop-same rep1 (round 1), graph-guard
loop-same rep2 (round 1), packaging loop-same rep4 (round 2). Only the packaging event was
disclosed. Makes the Intro sentence factually false, understates the paper's own central
thesis (the mechanism recurred three times), and omits an operational-failure component from
the graph-guard cells that 4.8 leans on (the plateau argument survives: all five graph-guard
replicates landed Delta_same = 5 whether or not a round was truncated). Also 5.2's "A
truncation at the first round of a cell fails that cell loudly as missing data" reads
awkwardly unless "first round" is explicitly the tester round (round 0).

B2. "18 of 20 critic rounds" on graph-guard -- receipts show 16 of 18. The five counted
graph-guard loop-same cells ran 4+4+4+3+3 = 18 critic rounds, of which 2 were rejected (the
two 32k truncations of B1). "Overwhelmingly accepted" survives at 16/18; the numbers as
written are wrong on both numerator and denominator.

B3. "on a different subject, months of engineering later" -- wrong on both counts. The
featured recurrence is on packaging, the same subject whose truncated loop-same critic rounds
were the autopsy's clearest Experiment-1 illustration; and the defect was repaired 2026-07-10
(protocol_version 9) with the recurrence dated 2026-07-13: three days, not months. Both facts
trivially checkable in the public repo the paper points readers to.

B4. Section 3.4 self-contradicting definition (the known class) still present in locked v4.
"Fails on the pristine module" is assigned two mutually exclusive outcomes in consecutive
sentences: whole-round rejection with zero kills vs individual salvage with the round
continuing. Actual instrument behavior: individual pristine-failing tests are pruned; the
round is rejected only when the file fails validation as a file (unparseable, truncated, or no
tests survive).
